void replace_labels ( asm_t *  asm_struct)

Replace the labels by their relative address.

Parameters
asm_structThe asm struct.
Warning
The labels must be NULL terminated.
Note
The labels called are replaced by their relative address.
The relative address is the difference between the label address and the call address.
The call address is the address of the instruction that calls the label.
